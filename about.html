Founder & CEO

Aravindan S

A visionary technology entrepreneur with over two decades of experience architecting enterprise-grade solutions for global organisations. Before founding Zacaya, Aravindan served as a senior architect at IBM and Infosys, where he led digital transformation initiatives for Fortune 500 clients across North America, Europe, and APAC.

An alumnus of IIT Madras, he combines deep technical expertise with a rare ability to translate complex technological possibilities into measurable business outcomes. Under his leadership, Zacaya has grown from a five-person startup into a trusted technology partner for 500+ enterprises across 40 countries — driven by a philosophy he calls Beyond Innovation: the belief that technology, when implemented with precision and purpose, can transform any organisation into an industry leader.

Aravindan is a sought-after speaker on AI-driven enterprise transformation, a mentor to emerging technology founders, and an active contributor to open-source enterprise architecture frameworks. He is based in Chennai, where Zacaya is headquartered.
