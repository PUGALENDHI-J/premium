Who We Are

A Legacy of Compassion

Shakthi Foundation was founded in 2018 by a group of dedicated social workers, educators, and entrepreneurs who witnessed the stark inequalities in Bangalore's urban communities. Seeing children denied education, families struggling for food, and talented individuals lacking opportunity — they resolved to act.

The name "Shakthi" means strength in Sanskrit — embodying our core belief that every human being possesses the innate power to rise, when given the right support, opportunity, and dignity.

Today, we operate across seven transformative programs, having impacted over 12,000 lives with a passionate network of 500+ volunteers, partner organizations, and generous donors.

Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & Values

🔭

Our Vision

A society where every individual — regardless of birth, circumstance, or background — has access to education, dignified employment, healthcare, and the opportunity to build a meaningful life.

"An India where no child is left behind, no family goes hungry, no potential is wasted."

🎯

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged individuals and communities through holistic, sustainable programs covering education, employment, entrepreneurship, healthcare, food security, clothing, shelter, and child welfare.

  • ✦ Provide equitable access to quality education
  • ✦ Create employment & entrepreneurship pathways
  • ✦ Ensure food, healthcare & basic welfare for all
  • ✦ Protect and uplift children in need

Our Journey

The Story So Far

Six years of relentless service — milestone by milestone, life by life.

2018 · FOUNDING

Shakthi Foundation Established

Founded in Bangalore by a team of 8 social workers and educators with a seed fund of ₹2 lakhs and a vision to serve 500 families in the first year.

2019 · EXPANSION

Education & Food Programs Launched

Opened first education center serving 120 children. Initiated weekly food distribution drives reaching 300 families across 3 localities in Bangalore.

2020 · RESILIENCE

COVID Relief & Digital Education

Distributed ration kits to 2,000+ families during lockdown. Launched digital learning program with donated devices for children unable to access school.

2021 · GROWTH

Healthcare & Women Empowerment

First free healthcare camp organized — 800 patients screened. Women's entrepreneurship program launched with 60 participants and ₹15 lakh in micro-grants disbursed.

2022 · SCALE

Child Welfare & Orphanage Support

Partnered with 12 orphanages across Karnataka. Launched child welfare program covering education, nutrition, mental health, and skill development for 400+ children.

2023 · IMPACT

10,000 Lives Milestone Achieved

Crossed the landmark of 10,000 direct beneficiaries. Secured major CSR partnerships with 3 Bangalore-based tech companies for sustained annual funding.

2024 · TODAY

12,000+ Lives & Growing

Operating 7 active programs, 500+ volunteers, and expanding into rural Karnataka districts. Laying groundwork for a dedicated Shakthi Foundation community center.
