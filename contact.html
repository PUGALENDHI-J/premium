Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message via the contact form above and our team will be happy to help.

"Every question you ask brings us one step closer to making a difference together."

How can I volunteer with Harini Foundation? ▾
You can volunteer by filling out the contact form on this page and selecting "Volunteer With Us" as the purpose. Our volunteer coordinator will reach out within 24 hours to guide you through the onboarding process. We welcome all skills and availability levels.
How do I make a donation to Harini Foundation? ▾
You can donate through the donation section on our Impact page, or contact us directly at info@harinifoundation.org to arrange a bank transfer, UPI, or cheque donation. All donations are acknowledged with receipts for tax purposes.
Is Harini Foundation a registered NGO? ▾
Yes, Harini Foundation is a registered non-governmental organisation (NGO) in India under the Societies Registration Act. We operate with full transparency and are compliant with all statutory requirements. Our registration documents are available on request.
Can my company partner with Harini Foundation for CSR? ▾
Absolutely. We actively work with corporate partners through CSR programmes, employee volunteering events, and in-kind support initiatives. Please select "Partner / CSR" in the contact form and our partnerships team will connect with you to explore the best fit.
Where does Harini Foundation operate? ▾
Our primary operations are based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. We serve communities across multiple districts within the city and have expanded outreach activities to surrounding areas. Our goal is to scale impact across Karnataka and eventually beyond.
How long does it take to receive a response? ▾
We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 business hours (Monday–Saturday, 10AM–6PM). For urgent matters, you are welcome to call us directly on our published phone number.
