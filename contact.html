FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How quickly can Zacaya start on my project?
+

For most projects, we can begin discovery and scoping within 5-7 business days of contract execution. Urgent engagements can often begin within 48 hours. Contact us to discuss your timeline requirements.

Do you work with companies outside India?
+

Yes — we actively serve clients across 40+ countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia, and across Europe. Our teams work across time zones and we have regional offices in Singapore, Dubai, and London.

What is your minimum project size?
+

We typically engage with projects starting from Rs 5 Lakhs for focused deliverables. For enterprise-scale transformations, our engagements commonly range from Rs 25 Lakhs to Rs 5 Crores+. We'd be happy to discuss what's appropriate for your specific needs.

Do you offer post-implementation support?
+

Absolutely. We offer 24x7 post-implementation support, maintenance retainers, and continuous optimisation programs. Our clients consider us long-term partners, not one-time vendors — our 98% retention rate reflects this.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?
+

Zacaya is ISO 27001 certified and follows strict data security protocols across all engagements. We comply with GDPR, PDPA, and relevant regional data protection frameworks. All client data is handled under NDA and our comprehensive security policies.

Can you work with our existing technology stack?
+

Yes. We specialise in integrating with existing enterprise systems, not replacing everything unnecessarily. Our architects conduct a thorough technology assessment before recommending any changes — maximising your existing investments while filling strategic gaps.
