✦ Our Journey

Six Years of Continuous Impact

From a small volunteer group to a registered NGO serving thousands, our growth reflects the trust communities have placed in us.

2018

Foundation Established

Shakthi Foundation was registered in Bangalore with a core team of 5 volunteers focused on education support for underprivileged children.

2019

First 100 Scholarships

Launched the first scholarship cohort, supporting 100 students across 8 government schools in Bangalore's north and east zones.

2020

COVID Relief Operations

Pivoted during the pandemic to distribute food kits to over 2,000 families and facilitated remote learning for 600+ students.

2022

Women Empowerment Wing

Launched dedicated entrepreneurship and skills training for women, enabling over 400 women to gain financial independence within 18 months.

2024

12,500+ Lives Impacted

Crossed a milestone of 12,500 direct beneficiaries across education, employment, healthcare, and social welfare programmes.
