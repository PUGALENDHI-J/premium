Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Hope & Humanity

Harini Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to transforming lives across underprivileged communities in Bangalore and beyond. Founded with the belief that every human deserves dignity, opportunity, and care.

We operate across seven key impact pillars — education, employment, entrepreneurship, food, clothing, healthcare, and child welfare — creating sustainable change from the ground up.

"Together, we transform failures into success — one life at a time."
