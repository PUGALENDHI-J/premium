Deep Dive

Program Details

📚
Program 01

Education Support

Ensuring every child has access to quality education — regardless of economic background. We believe education is the single most powerful tool for generational change.

  • Full and partial scholarships for school and college students
  • After-school tutoring centers in 6 Bangalore localities
  • Free school supplies, uniforms, and textbooks distribution
  • Digital literacy classes with donated laptops and tablets
  • Counseling for dropout prevention and student mental health
  • Career guidance and higher education application support
Support This Program →
💼
Program 02

Employment Support

Bridging the gap between opportunity and talent — our employment support program connects capable individuals with dignified, sustainable jobs.

  • Job placement assistance across 80+ partner employers
  • Resume writing, interview coaching, and grooming sessions
  • Industry-specific skill certification programs
  • Soft skills and workplace communication training
  • Internship placement for youth aged 18–25
  • Post-placement follow-up and workplace support
Support This Program →
🚀
Program 03

Entrepreneurship Development

We believe in teaching people to fish, not giving them fish. Our entrepreneurship program turns dreams into viable, sustainable businesses.

  • Micro-grants ranging from ₹10,000 to ₹1 lakh for startups
  • 12-week intensive business skills bootcamp
  • Women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs) formation and support
  • Market linkage support and product promotion
  • Financial literacy and bookkeeping workshops
  • Mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs and industry leaders
Support This Program →
🍱
Program 04

Food & Welfare Services

No child should sleep hungry. Our food programs ensure nutritious meals reach the most vulnerable families and communities across Bangalore.

  • Weekly food drives reaching 300+ families
  • Daily mid-day meal support for 5 community schools
  • Emergency ration kits during floods, drought, and crises
  • Festival community meals — Eid, Diwali, Christmas
  • Child nutrition programs with balanced diet awareness
  • Partnership with hotels and restaurants for surplus food redistribution
Support This Program →
🏥
Program 05

Medical & Healthcare Services

Health is wealth — and no community should be denied it. Our healthcare program brings professional medical care directly to underserved communities.

  • Quarterly free healthcare camps with specialist doctors
  • Blood pressure, diabetes, and cancer screening drives
  • Free prescription medicines through pharmacy partnerships
  • Mental health awareness workshops and counseling sessions
  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Eye check-up camps with free spectacles distribution
Support This Program →
👶
Program 06 & 07

Child Welfare & Shelter Support

Every child deserves love, safety, and opportunity. We work with orphanages, schools, and families to ensure no child is left behind.

  • Education and emotional support for 400+ orphaned children
  • Partnership with 12+ orphanages across Karnataka
  • Long-term mentorship programs for at-risk youth
  • Seasonal clothing drives — winter blankets, school uniforms
  • Emergency shelter access for homeless families
  • Transitional housing partnerships for displaced individuals
